Release v2.0

Released: June 2022

Notes

This update highlights designers across Red Hat from varied disciplines to get a realistic and in depth perspective on open design. The update features 8 individual designer interviews as individual pages, a design profiles overview page, and the navigational structure to support these new pages.

Impact

Highlights designers as individuals and emphasizes the importance of an open design culture and the effect it has on our work at Red Hat.

Added

  • Design profiles overview page
  • 8 designer profile pages
  • Release notes page
  • Nav structure

Updated

  • Updated home page content and links to designer profiles

Release v1.0

Released: October 2021

Notes

Design.redhat.com is a new, central location for design and designers at Red Hat. We hope this is a useful resource for our internal design teams, across all disciplines and teams, as well as a tool to introduce the design world at large to our open design approach.

Impact

Highlights the practice of design at Red Hat in all its forms, makes a bold and public statement on the importance of open, collaborative design practices, collects the resources you need to do your job in one place, and serves as an effective recruiting tool for new members of our teams.

Added

  • Main hub page for design at Red Hat including information on how we work, design resources, and design job information.
